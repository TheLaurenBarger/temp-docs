Badger Quest : Badger Animation

Created by Lauren Barger on Aug 26, 2022

I have a badger plushie, named Badger, and he’s gorgeous. This is an attempt to bring him to life with Blender. The idea is that by the end I’ll have a blender model that I can record in all manner of scenarios to bring some of my webpage ideas to life.

Badger sketches.

Drawing badgers is a tad trickier than I thought it would be. First of all, you need to draw the badger from multiple angles to really figure out what the 3D model would look like. Some pictures for reference:

But my badger is a little more on the fat side, so I don’t think this is at a finished state just yet. But as a first try exercise, I think this will be fine.
